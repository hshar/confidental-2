Elevate your dental practice with Confidental's Mookambika Dental Chair. This electric chair offers programmable options, advanced LED technology, and powerful suction, redefining the standard for modern and comfortable dental procedures.

SURGI Dental Operator's Stool

The "SURGI" Dental Operator's Stool ensures optimal comfort and mobility. Mookambika Dental chair ensures smooth mobility with five castors and a chromium-plated base. The stool's pneumatic piston allows easy height adjustment, catering to the ergonomic needs of dental professionals during procedures.

LED Operating Light

Illuminate your workspace with our advanced LED Operating Light. Boasting a multi-surface reflector with a capacity of 30,000 lux, it ensures optimal visibility for dental procedures. The non-touch sensor allows convenient on/off and intensity control, providing ease of use with its well-balanced arm for three-directional movement. This helps to capture the tiny details of the operative field with minimal effort.

Assistant Side Chair Console

Enhance your dental practice with the Assistant Side Chair Console of the Mookambika Dental Chair. This comprehensive console integrates high and low vacuum suction cannulas, providing optimal suction capabilities for various dental procedures. Additionally, the inclusion of a 3-way syringe ensures convenience and efficiency, making it a valuable asset for your dental workspace.

Modular Delivery Hanging system

Maximize efficiency in your dental practice with the Modular Delivery Hanging System. This system integrates essential tools seamlessly, including Airotor points, Supreme Micro Motor, optic scaler, 3-way syringe, Beecool Turbo II light cure, SS Instrument Tray, and an X-Ray viewer. With precision and efficiency, it enhances your workflow for superior patient care.

Multifunctional Foot control

Enhance your workflow with the Multifunctional Foot Control of the Mookambika Dental Chair. This versatile control allows seamless operation of Airotor and micromotor functions, along with a joystick feature for effortless chair movement. The integration of the Supreme Micromotor at 35,000 rpm, with a digital speed display ensures precision and efficiency in your dental procedures.
