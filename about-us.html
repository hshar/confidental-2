History

Apart from the world class machinery, one of the important asset is our workforce. Their abilities to use the machinery and work with precision is what helps us to deliver the best quality products to our clients. We have put together the best possible facilities to make sure they feel comfortable. Be it our production area, storage or inventory, we have maintained all the required standards.

1978-88 Beginning and Growth

In 1978, Dr. Shetty founded the Organization to supply top-notch Dental Equipment to Indian Dentists. Over a decade's perseverance led to a milestone in 1988 when it transformed from a Partnership to a Private Limited Company, marking significant growth in Dental Equipment manufacturing.

1988-98 Diversification

Confidental diversified its operations, along with dental products, we also started the manufacturing of medical equipments and emerged as a leader in the Indian Market.

1998-08 Became Public

In 2002, Confidental achieved another milestone by becoming a public limited company. We introduced Confident Dental Laboratory and Confident Sales India Pvt Ltd.

2008-18 Expanding Further

In 2009, Confident Dental Equipment implemented Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP). We also inaugurated a new building with a built up area of 2.25 lakh Sq.ft at Bidadi, Bangalore.

2018-Present Innovatiion & Growth

Confident thrived with its strategic leadership, notable product launches, and COVID-19 responsiveness. We introduced the Clean Cut Dental Laser, initiatives like Turnkey Projects and the Milling and Design Centre.
