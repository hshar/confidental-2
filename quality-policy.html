Accreditation and Certifications

Embarking on a mission to craft world-class dental units & medical products, Confident Dental Equipments Ltd. is at the forefront of quality initiatives within its manufacturing plant. Our commitment to delivering the highest standards is evident through comprehensive 5S techniques and an all-encompassing plan for total quality management.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Ensuring excellence, we are ISO 9001:2015 certified for the design, development, and manufacture of dental chair mount units, dental clinic and laboratory equipment, OT tables, lights, and hospital furniture.

ISO 13485:2016

Committed to healthcare precision, our ISO 13485:2016 certification encompasses the design, development, manufacture, installation, and servicing of a wide range of dental units, medical/surgical equipment, chairs, dental lab equipment, and more.

Flash X-Ray BIS Licence

Our commitment to safety is highlighted by our Flash X-Ray BIS Licence, covering medical electric equipment such as diagnostic X-Ray machines, high-frequency generators, and mobile options for comprehensive healthcare solutions.

CDSCO Certificate

Confident Dental proudly holds a CDSCO certification, ensuring our dental products adhere to stringent quality standards set by India's regulatory body. Trust in our commitment to safety and excellence in oral care.
