Electro Hydraulic OT Table

Description

Explore the efficiency of Confidental's Electro Hydraulic OT Table (CME-PD-001). With precise controls and versatile positioning, it ensures optimal patient comfort for seamless general Surgery and certain special procedures.

Highlights

Precision meets durability in Electro Hydraulic OT Table, ensuring smooth positioning, durability, and secure locking for optimal procedures.

Technical Specifications

Mechanical Adjustments
  • Raising and lowering of the table through Hydro-Mechanical operation
  • Backrest raising and lowering positions
  • Trendelenburg and Reverse trendelenburg positions
  • Lateral tilt left to right
  • Floor locking mechanism (optional)
  • Head Section – moved mechanically, detachable.
  • Simple leg rest with upward, downward movements
  • Hydraulic lift controlled by a foot pedal
  • Mechanical actuating system for smooth and rigid table movements
