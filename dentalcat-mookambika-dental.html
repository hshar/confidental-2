Mookambika Dental Chair

Description

Introducing Confidental's Mookambika Dental Chair, an electric, programmable chair with advanced LED and powerful suction, ensuring a superior dental experience.

Highlights

Elevate your practice with Mookambika Dental Chair – electric, rotatable, and packed with advanced features for precision dentistry.

LED Operating light

Light having 30000 lux on/off and intensity control by non touch sensor with 3 directional movement

DRYCO suction

High and low vacuum motorised “DRYCO” suction with auto drain and autoflush system

Programmable chair

Body contoured Electrically operated Dental chair with Zero program with Right arm rotatable

'SURGI' Dental operators stool

Stool moving on five castors with chromium plated base. Pneumatic piston for up down movement

Technical Specifications

Modular Delivery system
  • Airotor points with tubing & nut fitting - 2 Nos
  • Supreme Micro motor 35,000rpm with digital display of speed
  • SS Instrument Tray
  • Supreme optic scaler with five scaling tips
  • 3-way syringe
  • X -Ray viewer
Power requirements
  • Suction power of 350 Ltr / min.
  • Supreme Micro motor 35,000rpm with digital display of speed
